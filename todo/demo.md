# To-Do List App - Quick Demo

## Getting Started (2 minutes)

### Step 1: Open the App
1. Open `index.html` in any web browser
2. You should see a beautiful purple to-do list interface

### Step 2: Add Your First Task
1. Click in the input field that says "Add a new task..."
2. Type something like: "Buy groceries"
3. Press **Enter** or click the **ADD** button
4. Your task appears in the list!

### Step 3: Try All Features

**Add More Tasks:**
- Type "Finish project" and press Enter
- Type "Exercise 30 mins" and press Enter
- Type "Call mom" and press Enter

**Mark Tasks Complete:**
- Click the checkbox next to "Buy groceries"
- Notice it fades and gets a strikethrough
- The task counter updates!

**Filter Tasks:**
- Click **Active** to see only uncompleted tasks
- Click **Completed** to see finished tasks
- Click **All** to see everything

**Delete Tasks:**
- Click **Delete** next to any task to remove it
- Or click **Clear Completed** to delete all done tasks

### Step 4: Close and Reopen
1. **Close your browser tab** (or the whole browser)
2. Open the `index.html` file again
3. **All your tasks are still there!** ✨
   - This is the magic of local storage!

## Common Questions

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: In your browser's local storage. It's 100% private and never leaves your computer.

**Q: What if I clear my browser cache?**
A: Your tasks will be deleted. Back them up using the export method in the README.

**Q: Can I use this on mobile?**
A: Yes! Open `index.html` on your phone and it works perfectly.

**Q: Can multiple people use the same app?**
A: Each person using a different browser profile will have their own tasks.
